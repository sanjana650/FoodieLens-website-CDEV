About Us

FoodieLens is a gourmet lifestyle restaurant review website designed for bon vivants who share the belief that food is the ultimate universal language. We are on an enthusiastic quest to seek out the latest dining trends and uncover the dynamics and intricacies of the local culinary scene. FoodieLens uncovers the truth about restaurants so you can determine if the restaurant would suit you. Read honest reviews from diners who have gone to the restaurants and join our community
